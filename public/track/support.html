Common Questions

How do I add a project?

Tap the "+" button at the top right of the project list screen and fill in your project information.

What do the status colors mean?

Green (✓) = on track, Yellow (−) = at risk, Red (✕) = blocked. Tap the status icon to cycle through them.

How do I set a deadline reminder?

When creating or editing a project, set a deadline and choose how many days in advance you want to be reminded (same day, 1 day, 3 days, or 7 days).

How do I export my project list?

Open a project's detail view and tap the share button. You can send via email or save to Notes.

How do I change the app language?

Go to Settings and select your preferred language (Français or English).
