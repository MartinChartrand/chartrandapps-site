Common Questions

How do I add a credit card?

Tap the "+" button on the dashboard and follow the onboarding steps. You'll need your card's payment due date and credit limit.

How do I update my balance?

Tap on a card in the dashboard, then tap "Update Balance" and enter your current balance from your banking app or statement.

How do I mark a payment as complete?

When your card shows "Payment Due," tap the card and select "Mark Payment Complete."

What is the R7 countdown?

The R7 countdown shows how long until your consumer proposal or bankruptcy is purged from your credit report (typically 3 years from paid-off date). You can set this date in Settings.

How do I enable iCloud sync?

Go to Settings → Sync → Enable iCloud Sync. This will sync your data across all your devices signed in with the same Apple ID.

How do I unlock Premium features?

Tap "Unlock Premium" on the paywall screen to subscribe. Premium includes credit score tracking, projections, unlimited cards, and R7 countdown.

How do I restore my subscription?

Go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchase."

How do I delete all my data?

Go to Settings → Data → "Delete All Data." This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
