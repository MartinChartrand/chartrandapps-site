Privacy Policy

CreditCopilot · Effective: March 5, 2026 · Last updated: March 5, 2026

Your privacy is critical, especially when handling financial data. This Privacy Policy explains how CreditCopilot ("the App") handles your information. The short version: your financial data stays on your device and is never sent to us.

1. Information We Collect

CreditCopilot collects the following information that you provide:

  • Credit card information: card name, credit limit, balances, payment dates, and transaction history.
  • Credit score data (optional): credit score entries you manually enter for tracking purposes.

We do not collect or have access to your actual credit card numbers, CVVs, PINs, or any payment credentials. The App is a tracking tool, not a payment processor.

2. Data Storage

All data you enter into CreditCopilot is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

iCloud Sync (Optional): If you enable iCloud sync in the App's settings, your data is synced to your personal iCloud account via Apple's CloudKit service. This sync is:

  • Private and encrypted
  • Governed by Apple's Privacy Policy
  • Accessible only to you through your Apple ID
  • Stored on Apple's servers (which may be located outside Canada)

Chartrand Apps has no access to your iCloud data. We do not operate any servers that receive, process, or store your financial information.

3. Analytics

CreditCopilot uses TelemetryDeck, a privacy-focused analytics service, to understand how the App is used. TelemetryDeck:

  • Does not collect personally identifiable information (PII)
  • Uses anonymous session identifiers that rotate regularly
  • Is compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and Canadian privacy laws (PIPEDA/LPRPDE)

We collect anonymous usage data such as:

  • App launch events
  • Feature usage (e.g., "user added a card," without knowing which card or any details)
  • App version and device type (e.g., "iOS 17.3 on iPhone")

We do not collect: your credit card data, balances, payment dates, credit scores, or any financial information.

4. No Third-Party Tracking

CreditCopilot does not integrate any third-party advertising, user tracking, or data brokers. We do not use:

Firebase, Google Analytics, Facebook SDK, Mixpanel, Amplitude, Crashlytics, or any similar tracking service.

The App does not display ads and never will.

5. Push Notifications

CreditCopilot sends local push notifications to remind you of upcoming payment due dates and billing cycle milestones. These notifications are:

  • Scheduled entirely on-device
  • Do not involve any external servers
  • Can be managed in your device's Settings → Notifications → CreditCopilot

6. In-App Purchases

CreditCopilot offers an optional subscription ("CreditCopilot Premium") processed entirely by Apple's App Store. Chartrand Apps does not receive, store, or process any payment information. All purchase transactions are handled by Apple.

7. Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time:

  • Open the App → Settings → Data → "Delete All Data"
  • This action is permanent and cannot be undone
  • If iCloud sync is enabled, deleted data will also be removed from iCloud

8. Children's Privacy

CreditCopilot is designed for adults managing their credit and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Canadian Privacy Compliance

CreditCopilot complies with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and Quebec's Loi sur la protection des renseignements personnels dans le secteur privé (LPRPDE).

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (stored locally on your device)
  • Delete your data (via Settings → Delete All Data)
  • Export your data (via Settings → Export Data)
  • Disable iCloud sync (via Settings → Sync)

10. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Contact us at support@creditcopilot.ca.
