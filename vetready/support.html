Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I add a pet?

    Open VetReady and tap the + button on the My Pets screen. Enter your pet's name, select Dog or Cat, and optionally add a breed and photo. Tap Add to save.

  • How do I log a treatment?

    Tap on your pet from the main screen, then tap Log Treatment. Enter the product name, date given, and protection duration. VetReady will calculate when protection expires and schedule a reminder if you enable one.

  • How do I set up treatment reminders?

    When logging or editing a treatment, enable the Reminder toggle. You can also set a default reminder lead time in Settings. Reminders are sent as local push notifications ‚Äî no account or internet connection required.

  • Is my data backed up?

    Yes, if you have iCloud enabled on your device. VetReady syncs automatically with your personal iCloud account via Apple's CloudKit. If you reinstall the app or switch to a new iPhone, your data will restore from iCloud. Chartrand Apps never has access to your data.

  • How do I restore my purchase?

    Open VetReady and tap Add a second pet to reach the paywall, then tap Restore purchase at the bottom. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.
